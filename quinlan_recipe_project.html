Hot Chocolate Recipe

This recipe is super easy, and great for snow days!

Ingredients

  • Whole milk
  • Hersheys Dark Chocolate Syrup
  • Spoon
  • A mug of your choice
  • Marshmellows of any size
  • Cool whip

Directions

  1. Pour the milk into your mug.
  2. Put the mug into the microwave for 1 minute increments until at your desired temperature.
  3. Once the milk is at your desired temp. stir in one spoonful of the chocolate syrup.
  4. Once the syrup is fully stirred in add marshmellows.
  5. Finally Add your cool whip on top.

Cook time: 5 minutes

Serve hot, and this recipe is best enjoyed with friends :)
